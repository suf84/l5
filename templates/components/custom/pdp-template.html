Overview

LEVEL5 Drywall Skimming Blade Sets add that extra edge to your finishing tool arsenal. This 10/24/36" set configuration is an all-in-one drywall skimming tool set ideal for smoothing and finishing fresh compound on walls and ceilings.

  • This drywall skimming blade set includes all the tools you need to skim coat walls and ceilings.
  • Get fast and professional results when smoothing & finishing fresh compound on surfaces.
  • Quickly and easily apply a skim coat of joint compound after scraping popcorn ceilings.
  • The versatile extendable skimming blade handle provides just the right amount of additional reach.
  • Use the 10" skimming blade to wipe down fresh drywall tape on flat and butt joints.
